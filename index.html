  • For anyone that wants to progress in calisthenics skills like planche, front lever, iron cross and many more

How is the workout build?

After you fill in the questions about which goal you have and which exercises you are able to perform, the website will generate a workout for you. I programmed is so it knows what you have answered, what kind of exercises are good for your goal, it will implement the basics on the right day and if you don’t have a goal for example legs, it will implement extra exercises so you don’t have only one exercises to do. The cycle begins with more of a muscle conditioning and hypertrophy phase to build muscle, After that the intensity and difficulty will change and the workout will focus more on strength. If a week where you feel tired follows up a week where you felt really strong (after around 4-7 weeks), you want that week to be your deload-week. This number needs to be filled in in the last question. Note! The seconds that you need to fill in after you chose your progression are holds with good form. Also, a hold can depend on the day, make sure u are having that hold you are saying for at least 3 workouts in a row.

After you completed the workout cycle you should have an increased max reps of max hold of the skill you want to accomplish. Make with these numbers a new workout cycle with an improved number of seconds or even a higher progression.
